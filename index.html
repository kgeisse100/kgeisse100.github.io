About Me

Khristian Geisse is a guitarist, producer, and composer based in Los Angeles, California. His musical journey began as a teenager inspired by the guitar work of Jimi Hendrix, Stevie Ray Vaughn, and John Mayer.

Currently a senior at California Institute of The Arts pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Instrumental Arts: Guitar, his studies include Jazz, Indian classical music, and Persian classical music.
