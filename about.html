About Me

My Story

Khristian Geisse is a guitarist, producer, and composer based in Los Angeles, California. Khristian began his musical journey as a teenager inspired by the guitar work of Jimi Hendrix, Stevie Ray Vaughn, and John Mayer and began composing while still in high school.

As a composer and producer, Khristian has worked in a variety of styles ranging from R&B to electronic dance music and psychedelic rock producing for upcoming artists such as Capri Everitt, Breyan Bliss, and The Remedy in addition to his music being featured on Entertainment Tonight and After Midnight.

As a guitarist he has performed at the well-known annual 1500 or Nothin Day and has performed alongside Grammy-nominated artists India Shawn and Tish Hymanâ€”the latter for whom he serves as music director.

Currently, Khristian is a senior at California Institute of The Arts where he is pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Instrumental Arts: Guitar. His studies include Jazz, Indian classical music, and Persian classical music with professors such as Woody Aplanalp, Darek Oles, Houman Pourmhedi, Alam Khan, and Miroslav Tadic.

Musical Style & Influences

Khristian's music blends elements of jazz, R&B, and psychedelic rock, creating a unique sound that's both technically impressive and emotionally resonant. His guitar playing is characterized by melodic phrasing, rhythmic precision, and tasteful improvisation.

Key Influences

  • John Mayer - Guitar technique and songwriting
  • Jimi Hendrix - Innovative approach to the instrument
  • Stevie Ray Vaughan - Blues phrasing and intensity
  • D'Angelo - Neo-soul harmony and production
  • Thundercat - Modern jazz fusion elements
Education & Training

At California Institute of The Arts, Khristian has expanded his musical vocabulary through intensive study of:

  1. Jazz harmony and improvisation
  2. North Indian classical music
  3. Persian classical music
  4. Advanced music production techniques
  5. Composition for film and media
Equipment & Setup

Khristian uses a carefully selected collection of instruments and gear to achieve his signature sound, including:

  • Fender Stratocaster (main guitar)
  • PRS Custom 24 (alternate guitar)
  • Fender Twin Reverb amplifier
  • Ableton Live (primary DAW)
  • Various boutique effects pedals
